# Solar Racing Regulations

A structured, community-maintained archive of regulations, rulebooks, and related documents for solar car racing events around the world.

This repository is intended to make solar racing regulations easier to find, compare, reference, and preserve. It is especially useful for solar car teams, race organizers, scrutineers, engineering leads, students, researchers, and anyone studying the technical and sporting requirements of solar vehicle competitions.

## Why this repository exists

Solar racing regulations are often published by different organizations, in different formats, and across different websites. Older versions can be difficult to locate once new editions are released, even though historical regulations are valuable for engineering decisions, rule evolution studies, team onboarding, and cross-competition comparisons.

This repository aims to provide a single, organized reference point for solar racing regulations from major competitions.
